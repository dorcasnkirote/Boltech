Safe Browsing & Internet Security

What to Do for Safe Browsing

To ensure your safety while browsing the internet, it's important to follow these key tips:

  • Always use secure, encrypted websites (look for HTTPS in the URL).
  • Keep your browser and antivirus software up to date.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for different websites.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • Be cautious when entering personal information on unfamiliar websites.

What to Avoid

  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or pop-ups.
  • Never download files from untrusted sources.
  • Don’t share your personal information unless you’re sure about the site’s legitimacy.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions without a VPN.
  • Don’t ignore warnings from your browser or antivirus about a website’s security.
