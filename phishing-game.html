Phishing Awareness: How to Identify Phishing Emails

Phishing emails are fraudulent attempts to acquire sensitive information by pretending to be a trustworthy entity. In this game, you'll learn how to spot phishing emails and improve your cybersecurity awareness.

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate institutions via email to steal sensitive data such as login credentials, financial information, or personal details.

Example of a Phishing Email

Subject: Urgent: Your Account Has Been Suspended!

From: support@bankingalerts.com

Message: Dear User, your account has been suspended due to suspicious activities. Click here to verify your account and restore access immediately.

Red Flags: Look for strange email addresses, unexpected attachments, urgent messages asking for personal info, and suspicious links.

Alert! Always verify the sender's email address and avoid clicking on links from untrusted sources.
